    At ABNB, we want to help you meet your savings goals. A money market account lets you not only meet your goals but exceed them with competitive dividends that grow your balance automatically. 

    The beauty of a money market? You enjoy liquidity with access to your funds. So you can dip in when you need to without sacrificing your competitive rate.
